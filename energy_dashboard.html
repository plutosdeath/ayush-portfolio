03 · The trading thesis
01
Supply shock accelerates the transition
$100+ oil makes European biomethane cost-competitive almost overnight. Energy security concerns give politicians the mandate to deliver the regulatory framework the sector has been waiting for. Crisis becomes catalyst.
02
Certificate markets deepen and become tradeable
As biomethane scales toward the 350 TWh REPowerEU target, Guarantees of Origin and Proof of Sustainability certificates become liquid instruments. Cross-commodity expertise across EUAs, biofuels, and renewable gas becomes structurally valuable.
03
Compliance buyers need sophisticated counterparties
Shipping, industry, and power producers facing cost pressure from both oil prices and carbon obligations need flexible hedging solutions. Market makers with cross-commodity reach and deep regulatory knowledge capture the spread.
